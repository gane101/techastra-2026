About TechAstra 3.0

TechAstra 3.0 is the Annual Tech Fest of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj University, bringing together Innovation, Creativity, and Technology. Join us for an exciting journey through Workshops, Competitions, and Breakthrough Innovations.

Events

Hackathons & Coding Competitions

Build, code, and compete in intense coding challenges, hackathons, and programming battles with real-world problem statements and industry mentors.

Tech Workshops & Bootcamps

Hands-on workshops and bootcamps on AI/ML, web development, Python programming, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and emerging technologies led by industry experts.

Innovation & Project Showcase

Present your tech projects, startup prototypes, and innovations to a panel of industry judges, mentors, and peers. Win prizes and get noticed.

Tech Quiz & Knowledge Battle

Test your knowledge in fast-paced tech quizzes covering data structures, algorithms, CS fundamentals, current tech trends, and more.

Gaming Arena & E-Sports

Competitive gaming tournaments, e-sports battles, and virtual challenges — show off your reflexes, strategy, and team coordination.

& Much More...

Surprise events, guest speaker sessions, networking opportunities, startup exhibitions, and much more to be announced! Follow IIEC on social media for updates.
